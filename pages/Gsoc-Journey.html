I have started writing this blog today as I know that if I dont get selected for Gsoc 2023, I will probably never write it, and whenever I read a blog about Gsoc, it is mostly from a participant, and no one who tried very hard and didnt get selected tell about their mistakes. So, I will start with my journey

Gsoc 2022:

I knew about Gsoc well before it, and I always wanted to participate in it, but I was not that serious during that time as our university just called in december 2021 and literally did nothing except enjoying my college life as it was due for so long. I always wanted to try for Gsoc from inside but I never know how that time passed! I found out an organisation AnkiAndroid at that time as I have already done a bit of android, but trust me I never applied my knowledge of Android for solving any issue which I solved over there. So year passed by and now again that time comes.

Gsoc 2023:

I made my first priority as AnkiAndroid as I was comfortable with it by that time but to my surprise I come to know that they are not going to apply for Gsoc this year. Now I was in a real problem, I have still not decided any organisation and I have to first start find out a organisation and then contribute to them a bit. This time was a bit of down for me as I was having nothing in my hand but I was strong for atleast try out this time and I believe this time was one of a great learning lesson for me too, I find out a lot of organisations that I find a bit interesting, probably 7-8, joined their talking groups.
